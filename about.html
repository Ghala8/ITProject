About UniSlots Platform

Many students face difficulties in coordinating with Dr. Nawal during office hours, leading to congestion and chaos in prioritizing their needs.

Our platform, UniSlots, aims to organize the booking process in an easy and quick electronic way, allowing each student to book an appointment that suits them during Dr. Nawal's office hours without the need for prior attendance or waiting.

Problems

  1. Overcrowding during office hours causes confusion and delays.
  2. Difficulty in managing which student should be prioritized.
  3. Students are unaware of available time slots.
  4. Students often forget their scheduled appointments.
  5. No-shows waste valuable time for both students and Dr. Nawal.
  6. No proper record of student visits or discussion topics.

Solutions

  1. UniSlots allows students to book specific time slots, eliminating overcrowding.
  2. The system follows a first-come, first-served rule with timestamped bookings to manage priority.
  3. A live calendar view displays all available and booked slots in real time.
  4. Automatic reminders are sent via email or SMS before the appointment time.
  5. The platform includes a cancellation feature so others can use freed-up slots.
  6. Each appointment is logged and can include notes about the purpose of the visit for future reference.
